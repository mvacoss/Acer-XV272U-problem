# Acer-XV272U-problem
A friend of mine gave me monitor and said it wasnt working. He told me that it had something to do with power supply. I said okay, I can look at that and opened it up. I was immediatelly punched in face with a strong smell of vape liquid. The amount of it inside the monitor was like crazy. It was everywhere. I couldn't believe of how much of it was there. Of course it was the issue, since vape liquids are kind of conductive and when the fumes condense on the main PCB, they can cause short circuit. Which will result in not properly working monitor if you are lucky. If you arent, it can possibly cause fire. So if you kids don't want to have your monitors destroyed after a few years of daily use, please stop vaping in front of them. The monitors and any other electronics will thank you a lot.
